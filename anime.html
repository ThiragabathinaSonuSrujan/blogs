Death Note

Death Note is a Japanese anime television series adapted from the manga of the same name by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata. The show aired from October 2006 to June 2007 and became a global hit for its blend of psychological thriller, supernatural elements, and moral tension surrounding justice and power.

Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is a Japanese dark fantasy anime television series based on the manga by Sui Ishida. Premiering in 2014, it depicts a modern Tokyo inhabited by flesh-eating ghouls who hide among humans. The show gained international recognition for its visceral action, psychological themes, and tragic protagonist.

Steins;Gate

/Steins;Gate is a 2011 Japanese anime television series produced by White Fox, adapted from the visual novel of the same name by 5pb. and Nitroplus. Blending science fiction, thriller, and character drama, it explores time travel and its consequences through the perspective of eccentric college students in Akihabara.

Monster

Monster is a 2004 Japanese anime television series based on the manga by Naoki Urasawa. It follows a psychological thriller narrative centered on morality, identity, and the consequences of one’s choices. Widely acclaimed for its intricate plot and realistic tone, it is regarded as one of the standout anime of the 2000s.
